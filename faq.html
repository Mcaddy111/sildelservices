Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of events do you cater to?

We provide services for various events, including weddings, funerals, corporate events, concerts, and private parties. Our equipment is suitable for all sizes and types of gatherings.

2. How do I book a rental?

You can book by filling out our contact form, calling us directly, or emailing us at kelvimcaddy@gmail.com. Our team will assist you in finding the perfect package for your event.

3. Are delivery and setup included?

Yes, we provide delivery and setup as part of our rental packages. Our team will ensure everything is ready before your event starts.

4. Can I customize my rental package?

Absolutely! We understand that every event is unique. Our team will work with you to create a tailored rental package that fits your specific requirements and budget.

5. What if I need assistance during my event?

Our team can provide on-site technical support during your event to ensure everything runs smoothly. Just let us know your needs when booking.

6. Do you offer discounts for large events?

Yes, we offer competitive pricing and discounts for large events. Please contact us for a customized quote based on your specific requirements.
